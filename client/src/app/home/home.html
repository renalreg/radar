Rare Disease Registry

Welcome to the national Rare Disease Registry (RaDaR). In partnership with Rare Diease Groups (RDGs), RaDaR facilitates translational and epidemiological research into rare diseases through a comprehensive clinical database. RaDaR is managed by the UK Renal Registry on behalf of the Renal Association.

Recruitment to RaDaR is open to all UK hospitals. If your Trust would like to take part please contact melanie.dillon@renalregistry.nhs.uk. If your your Trust is already taking part and you require access to RaDaR please contact your research team.

To confirm a patient's eligibility and access consent documents please visit RareRenal.org.

Patients can view their data on PatientView.

For technical support please contact nbn-tr.radar@nhs.net.
